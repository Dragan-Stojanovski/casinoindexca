What is slot RTP?

RTP (Return to Player) is the percentage of all wagers a slot returns over the long run. A 96% RTP slot returns $96 per $100 wagered on average, with the 4% being the house edge. Higher RTP means better long-term value; always check it before you spin.

What slot volatility should I choose?

Low-volatility slots pay smaller wins more often — ideal for longer sessions and smaller bankrolls. High-volatility slots pay rarely but larger, suiting big-win hunters with a deeper bankroll and smaller bets. Match volatility to your budget and goals.
