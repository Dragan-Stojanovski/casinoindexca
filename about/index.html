Who we are

CasinoIndexCA is a Canadian-focused editorial team of casino analysts, payments specialists and responsible-gambling researchers. We publish independent audits of the online casinos that accept Canadian players — both offshore operators serving the rest of Canada and brands registered with iGaming Ontario. Our purpose is simple: replace the guesswork Canadians face when they deposit real money online with a measured, repeatable, published score we call the Safety Index.

We are readers' advocates first. Everything we publish is written to answer the questions a careful player actually asks before signing up — is this casino licensed, will it pay me quickly, are the bonus terms fair, and what happens if something goes wrong?

Why we exist

Most casino "review" sites are ranked by whoever pays the most. We think that gets it backwards. Canadians deposit real money with operators they have no easy way to vet — offshore licences, opaque terms, and withdrawal times that only reveal themselves after you've already won. CasinoIndexCA turns that guesswork into a measured, repeatable score, and we show our work: every rating links to the reasoning behind it.

How we review a casino

Every operator goes through the same six-stage process before it earns a Safety Index score. No shortcuts, and no exceptions for advertisers.

Shortlist & licence check

We confirm the operator accepts Canadian players and verify its licence and operating company against the issuing regulator's public register.

Fund a real account

We register and deposit our own money using Canadian methods — Interac, cards or crypto — and keep the dated receipt. No demo accounts.

Test the payout

We request a real withdrawal, time it end-to-end, and quiz live chat on cashout policy, KYC and limits.

Read every term

Wagering, max-bet rules, win caps, game weighting, dormancy fees and payment exclusions — read line by line from the operator's own documents.

Score & second-editor check

The four weighted pillars are scored, then a second editor fact-checks the review before it can be published.

Publish & maintain

Each review is dated and revisited as offers, terms and licences change. Any material change can move a score or trigger a flag at any time.

How the Safety Index works

Every operator is scored out of 10 against four weighted pillars. The weights are fixed and applied identically to every casino, whether we have a commercial relationship with them or not.

Licensing & Trust

35%

Which regulator licenses the operator, that licence's standing and enforcement history, corporate transparency, and the brand's track record and longevity.

Payout Reliability

30%

How quickly and predictably an operator pays. We look at observed withdrawal times across Interac, e-wallets, cards and crypto, plus limits and cashout friction.

Terms Integrity

20%

Wagering requirements, max-bet rules, win caps, game weighting, dormancy fees and payment exclusions — rewarding operators whose terms are fair and clearly published.

Dispute History

15%

Complaint volume relative to size, and — more importantly — how the operator resolves disputes. A clean record lifts the score; unpaid winnings sink it.

Editorial standards

We hold every page on this site to the same standards, because trust is the only thing a review site is really selling.

  • Named authors. Every review is written by a named member of our team and reviewed by a second editor. The byline and fact-checker appear at the top of each page.
  • Primary sources. Licensing details, company registration and bonus terms come from the operator's own published terms and the relevant regulator — not from other review sites.
  • Fixed methodology. The same scoring model is applied to every operator. No operator sees or influences its score before publication.
  • Transparent flaws. When a casino has strict terms, low limits or undisclosed licensing, we say so plainly — even for operators we earn commission from.
  • Dated & maintained. Reviews carry a last-updated date and are revisited as offers, terms and licences change.

How we're funded

CasinoIndexCA is free for readers because we earn affiliate commissions from some of the operators we list. Those commercial relationships are recorded in a separate ledger and carry zero weight in the Safety Index. An operator cannot buy a higher score, a better position, or the removal of a flag. If an operator we're paid by fails an audit, it gets flagged. The data decides — every time.
