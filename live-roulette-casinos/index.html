Choose the right wheel — it halves the edge

This is the single most important thing to know about roulette. A European (single-zero) wheel has a house edge of about 2.7%; an American (double-zero) wheel is nearly double at 5.26%. French roulette with the La Partage rule can be even better on even-money bets (~1.35%). Always choose single-zero, and prefer French where available — it's free odds, simply by selecting the right table.

What is Lightning Roulette?

Lightning Roulette is Evolution's hit format that adds random multipliers up to 500x on straight-up numbers each round. It's more exciting but carries a slightly higher house edge than standard European roulette, so it trades odds for thrill.

What is Immersive Roulette?

Immersive Roulette is an Evolution table filmed with multiple cameras and slow-motion replays of the ball landing, for a cinematic experience. The odds are standard European roulette; the difference is production quality.
