What is a reload bonus?

A reload bonus is a deposit match for existing players — a recurring promotion that tops up your account with extra bonus funds when you deposit again. Where a welcome bonus is a one-time hello, a reload is the casino's way of keeping you playing: match your Tuesday deposit 50%, add a Weekend Reload, or run a daily reload you can claim again and again. Individually they're smaller than a welcome offer, but because they repeat, their value compounds for regular players. Reloads vs welcome bonuses vs cashback

These three make up most of a casino's promotional value, and they serve different roles. A welcome bonus is a large one-time boost for new players. A reload is a smaller, repeatable match that rewards you for coming back. Cashback softens losing sessions and usually carries the lowest wagering. The best long-term value for a regular player is a casino that combines all three well — a fair welcome to start, ongoing reloads to keep topping up, and cashback as a safety net.
