Privacy Policy

How CasinoIndexCA handles the limited data we collect. Last updated: 2026.

Who we are

CasinoIndexCA ("we", "us", "our") operates an independent, informational comparison website about online casinos serving Canadian players. We are the data controller for the limited personal information described in this policy. This policy explains what we collect, why, how we protect it, and the rights you have under Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable provincial privacy legislation. It applies to this website only — not to the third-party casino operators we link to, each of which has its own privacy policy that governs any account you open with them.

Information we collect

We run a static informational site and deliberately collect as little as possible. The categories are:

  • Technical & usage data — such as anonymised analytics (pages viewed, approximate region, device and browser type) and standard server logs. This helps us understand how the site is used and improve it.
  • Information you provide — anything you voluntarily send us, for example your name, email and message when you use our contact form or report a correction.
  • Cookies & similar technologies — small files that support analytics and affiliate attribution, described below.

We do not ask for, and do not want, sensitive financial information. Deposits, withdrawals and identity verification happen entirely on the operator's own platform, never on this site.

Cookies & affiliate tracking

We use cookies for two purposes. First, analytics cookies help us measure aggregate traffic and improve the site. Second, when you click through to an operator, an affiliate-attribution cookie may credit that referral to us so we can be paid a commission. These attribution cookies do not change the price, bonus or terms you receive from the operator — you get exactly the same offer whether you arrive through us or directly. You can control or block cookies through your browser settings; blocking them will not stop you using the site.

Why we use your information (and our lawful basis)

  • To understand and improve how the site is used (legitimate interest, based on aggregated/anonymised data).
  • To respond to messages, correction reports and enquiries you send us (to fulfil your request).
  • To measure affiliate referrals to the operators we list (legitimate interest, to fund a free service).

Under PIPEDA we rely on your consent and our legitimate business interests, and we limit collection to what is reasonably necessary for these purposes.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. Limited data may be processed on our behalf by trusted service providers — for example analytics, website hosting, contact-form handling and affiliate networks — under their own privacy terms and only to deliver the services above. Some of these providers may process data outside Canada; where that happens, comparable protections apply. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purpose it was collected. Contact-form messages are retained while we handle your enquiry and for a reasonable period afterward; anonymised analytics are retained in aggregate. When information is no longer needed, we delete or anonymise it.

Security

The site is served over encrypted HTTPS, and we apply reasonable technical and organisational safeguards to protect the limited data we hold. No method of transmission over the internet is perfectly secure, but because we collect so little personal information, your exposure through this site is minimal.

Your privacy rights

Depending on your province, you have rights to access the personal information we hold about you, to request correction of inaccuracies, to withdraw consent, and in some cases to request deletion. To exercise any of these, contact us using the details below and we will respond within the timeframes required by applicable law. You also have the right to complain to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada, or your provincial privacy regulator.

Children

This site and the operators we list are strictly for adults aged 19+ (or the legal gambling age in your province). We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the legal age, and we do not knowingly present promotional content to minors. If you believe a minor has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Third-party links

Our site links to casino operators and other third parties. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices. When you leave our site and open an operator's site or account, that operator's privacy policy — not ours — governs the personal and financial information you provide to them. We encourage you to read it before registering.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes to our practices or the law. The "last updated" date at the top of the page shows the current version; material changes will be reflected there.

Contact us

Questions, requests or complaints about privacy: privacy@casinoindexca.com. You can also use our contact page. For how we handle editorial accuracy and corrections, see our editorial principles.
