What are Megaways slots?

Megaways is a game engine invented by Big Time Gaming that changed slots forever. Instead of fixed paylines, the number of symbols on each of the six reels changes randomly every spin — anywhere from two to seven — creating up to 117,649 ways to win (that's 67). Combined with cascading reels, where winning symbols vanish and new ones drop in for chain wins, and escalating multipliers in free spins, Megaways delivers a high-energy, high-variance experience with genuinely enormous win potential. Big Time Gaming now licenses the mechanic to studios like Pragmatic Play, Blueprint and Red Tiger, so the best casinos stock hundreds of Megaways titles.
