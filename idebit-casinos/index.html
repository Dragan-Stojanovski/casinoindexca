What is iDebit and how it works

iDebit is a Canadian online-banking payment service that lets you fund a casino account securely, straight from your bank, without ever exposing your banking login or account numbers to the operator. It works as a secure checkout layer: when you choose iDebit at the cashier, you're taken to authorise the payment inside your own bank's environment, then returned to the casino with the deposit complete. No credit card is involved, and there's no e-wallet balance to load first. For privacy-conscious Canadian players, that combination — bank-direct, card-free and with your details kept hidden — is exactly the appeal.

Why Canadians choose iDebit

  • Privacy. The casino never sees your banking credentials — iDebit sits in between as a secure gateway.
  • No card needed. It draws from your bank directly, so there's no credit card and none of the gambling-transaction blocks some banks apply to cards.
  • Instant deposits. Funds arrive immediately, with no wallet to pre-fund.
  • Canadian-built. Designed for the Canadian banking system, so it "just works" with major banks.
