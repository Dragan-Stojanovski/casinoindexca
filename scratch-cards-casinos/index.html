What are online scratch cards?

Online scratch cards are digital instant-win games — the online cousin of the physical scratch tickets you'd buy at a corner store. You buy a card, reveal the hidden symbols (scratch them one by one for suspense, or hit "reveal all"), and win if you uncover the matching combination shown in the rules. The result is instant and the games are dead simple, with themes spanning fruit, treasure, animals and branded slots. Their charm is the immediate hit of anticipation and a shot at a large payout from a small stake — no strategy, no waiting, just the reveal.
