Stablecoins: crypto speed without the rollercoaster

The biggest hesitation Canadians have about crypto is price volatility — nobody wants a $500 win to become $430 by the time they cash out. Stablecoins solve this. A coin like Tether (USDT) is pegged to hold a value close to the US dollar, so 100 USDT is worth roughly 100 USD whether you withdraw today or next week. You keep all the speed and low fees of crypto without the wild swings of Bitcoin's price. For most players who just want fast, reliable payouts rather than a bet on the crypto market, a stablecoin is the smart default.
