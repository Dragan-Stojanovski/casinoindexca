About the agency

We audit the operators. Nobody audits our score.

CasinoIndexCA is an independent consumer-auditing agency built for one job: telling Canadian players which real-money casinos are safe, fast and fair — measured, not marketed.

Why we exist

Most casino "review" sites are ranked by whoever pays the most. We think that gets it backwards. Canadians deposit real money with operators they have no easy way to vet — offshore licences, opaque terms, and withdrawal times that only reveal themselves after you've already won. CasinoIndexCA turns that guesswork into a measured, repeatable score.

How we stay independent

We do earn affiliate commissions from some of the operators we list — that keeps the platform free for readers. But those commercial relationships are recorded in a separate ledger and carry zero weight in the Safety Index. An operator cannot buy a higher score, a better position, or the removal of a flag. The same published methodology is applied to every operator, whether we have a commercial relationship with them or not.

Our commitment

If an operator we're paid by fails an audit, it gets flagged. If an operator we earn nothing from tops the index, it ranks first. The data decides — every time.

What we measure

  • Licensing integrity — regulator, standing, and enforcement history verified at source.
  • Payout reliability — real withdrawals timed and logged across payment methods.
  • Terms integrity — wagering requirements, caps and fine print read line by line.
  • Dispute history — complaint volume and how operators resolve them.

Regional compliance

Ontario runs its own regulated iGaming market under the AGCO and iGaming Ontario, with advertising standards that restrict how bonuses and inducements are shown to the public. When a visitor selects the Ontario region, our site follows those standards automatically — showing licensing, registration and verified payout data instead of promotional figures.
