What is a first deposit bonus?

A first deposit bonus is the simplest and most transparent welcome offer: the casino matches a percentage of your opening deposit in bonus funds. A 100% match on a $100 deposit hands you $100 in bonus money, giving a $300 total balance to play with. Push the match higher — 200% doubles your deposit in bonus, 300% triples it — and the bonus grows accordingly. Because it's a single, clearly defined match rather than a sprawling multi-deposit package, a first deposit bonus is the easiest offer to understand and, crucially, to judge. This page ranks the best 100%, 200% and 300% matches for Canadians and shows you exactly what each is worth.

100% vs 200% vs 300% — what changes

A bigger match percentage always means more bonus funds, but it rarely comes for free. Higher matches typically pair with higher wagering requirements and sometimes lower maximum deposits, so the extra bonus is harder to convert into withdrawable cash. As the calculator above shows, a 100% match at 25× can end up worth more than a 300% match at 45×, because the cost of clearing all that wagering — the house edge on every forced bet — climbs with the number. The right choice depends on how much you intend to play: if you'll grind the wagering, a bigger match rewards you; if you want your winnings quickly, a smaller, lower-wagering match is smarter.

The terms that decide a match's real value

Beyond the match percentage, four clauses determine what you actually keep. The wagering multiplier and whether it applies to bonus only or deposit-plus-bonus is the biggest factor. The maximum bet while wagering (often capped around $5) can void the bonus if breached. The maximum cashout can limit winnings from the bonus. And game weighting decides which games count. A "300%" headline means little until you've read these; the calculator above turns them into a single real-value figure so you can compare offers honestly.

What is a first deposit bonus?

A first deposit bonus matches a percentage of your opening deposit in bonus funds. A 100% match on a $100 deposit gives you $100 extra to play with; a 200% match gives $200. It's the simplest, most transparent form of welcome offer.

What does a 200% or 300% deposit match mean?

A 200% match doubles your deposit in bonus funds — deposit $100 and receive $200 bonus for $300 total. A 300% match triples it. Bitkingz, for example, offers an exclusive 200% + 200 spins and a separate 300% first-deposit code. Higher matches usually carry wagering, so check the terms.

Is a 300% deposit match better than 100%?

Not automatically. A higher match gives more bonus funds but usually comes with higher wagering and often lower maximum deposits. A 100% match at low wagering can be easier to clear and better value than a 300% match at 45×. Use the calculator above to compare real value.

How do I claim a first deposit bonus?

Register, opt in to the offer (or enter the bonus code where required), and make a qualifying deposit above the minimum. The bonus is credited automatically. Some offers, like Bitkingz's 300% match, use a specific code such as BK300 for the first deposit only.

Do first deposit bonuses have wagering?

Usually yes — commonly 35×–45× on the bonus. You must bet the bonus that many times before winnings are withdrawable. Always check the multiplier, whether it applies to bonus only or deposit plus bonus, and any maximum bet or win cap.

What is the minimum deposit to claim a match?

It varies but a qualifying minimum of around $20–$30 is common, sometimes with a specified minimum for a particular match tier. Bitkingz's 300% offer, for instance, has its own minimum. Always check the offer's qualifying deposit before funding.
